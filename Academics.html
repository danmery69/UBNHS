Mission

Upper Bicutan National High School is dedicated to providing quality education that is accessible, relevant, and inclusive for all learners. :

  • e are committed to developing the intellectual, emotional, and social skills of our students by offering a diverse and engaging curriculum that prepares them for future challenges.
  • Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.
  • Through collaboration with parents, the community, and dedicated educators, we aim to create an environment where students can grow as responsible, respectful, and empowered individuals who are ready to contribute positively to society.
  • We will continue to uphold the values of excellence, integrity, and service as we work towards building a brighter future for our learners and the community.

Vision

At Upper Bicutan National High School, we envision a learning community where every student is empowered to excel academically, socially, and personally.
